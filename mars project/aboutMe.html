About This Website

This website explores space-related topics using NASA's APIs. You'll find:

  • NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) with a description.
  • Images taken by Mars rovers during their missions.
  • Weather data from Mars, straight from NASA's Mars Weather API.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy exploring space with me!
